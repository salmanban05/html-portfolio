Top Philosophical Books by Salman

The most mind-blowing books in pop philosophy that are approachable by anyone.

  • Animal Farm [by George Orwell]

    • An allegory of the Communist revolution of Russia, this book explores the subtle way in which society is altered by the powerful to corrupt it.

  • Nineteen Eighty Four [by George Orwell]

    • Yet another masterpiece by Orwell, this book explores the open secret of human soceity, authoritarianism, and the fear, lies and deception required to keep the common man complacent in it.

  • A Brave New World [by Aldous Huxley]

    • Touted to be the anti-thesis of Nineteen Eighty Four, this book delves into the more subtle form of authoritarianism...
    The kind that controls the masses by numbing them with pleasure and distractions.
